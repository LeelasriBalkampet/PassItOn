At PassItOn

we are on a mission to transform the way we view and handle surplus resources. Every year, tons of food and other essentials go to waste while countless individuals face scarcity. We're here to bridge that gap, fostering a culture of sharing and sustainability. We started with a simple question: What if surplus could create value instead of waste? This idea grew into a platform where individuals, businesses, and communities unite to share resources, reduce waste, and spread kindness.

Who We Are

We are more than a platform; we are a movement toward conscious living. Our team comprises dedicated individuals, passionate volunteers, and innovative thinkers who believe in the power of collaboration. Together, we work to minimize food wastage, support the needy, and promote mindful consumption habits.

We understand that small actions lead to big changes. By creating a seamless way for people and businesses to share what they don't need, we're making sustainable living accessible to everyone.
